PhD Candidate, Illinois Institute of technology

He graduated with an M.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Piraeus in Athens. He was awarded a scholarship for graduating 2nd in my class.

He moved to the US to pursue my doctoral studies in Computer Science. He joined Illinois Institute of Technology in August 2013.
